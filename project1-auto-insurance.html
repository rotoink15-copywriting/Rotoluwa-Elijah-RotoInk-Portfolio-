Overview

A digital auto insurance startup needed clearer messaging to help users understand how digital-first coverage works compared to traditional insurance.

❌ The Problem

Users didn't understand the difference between traditional insurance and digital-first coverage. The page was filled with jargon, unclear benefits, and no strong direction toward conversion.

✓ My Approach

I redesigned the hero section, clarified the value proposition, simplified benefits, and created a clean comparison block supported by a strong CTA flow.

My Approach

  • Redesigned the hero section with a clear, benefit-driven headline
  • Clarified the value proposition in simple, jargon-free language
  • Simplified benefits to focus on user pain points
  • Created a clean comparison block showing digital-first advantages
  • Developed a strong CTA flow that removes friction

The Final Copy

Get your auto insurance registered in just 3 minutes. Yes, it's that easy.

Welcome to a smarter, digital-first auto insurance experience designed specifically for young drivers like you. No paperwork. No long queues. No stress.

Tap the button and get started

You don't want to miss how simple this really is.

The Results

The simplified messaging reduced confusion, improved engagement on the hero section, and helped users instantly grasp the benefits.
